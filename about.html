About Me

Your trusted navigator of the college admissions journey.

My Story

I am Hemamalini (Hema) Sathish, educated in India and living in California for the past 20+ years. When it was time to apply to college for my oldest, everything was new and I had to learn, research and realized the length and details of the process, and really appreciated the support a college admissions counselor offered during the process.

The ever changing landscape of college admissions makes navigating it a challenge, so I decided to pursue learning about it and graduated with an Independent Education Consultant Certificate from The University of California, Irvine. I am also a member of NACAC (National Association for College Admissions Counseling). Having enjoyed the support of a counselor, I hope to bring the same interest and passion and help to empower and de-stress students and their families during the entire process from starting it to making final decisions.

Hemamalini Sathish
